by Peter Cole - Thursday, 15 May 2025, 4:05 PM
Number of replies: 0

Hi All

I have asked that if a team wants a meeting with me to just book a teams Meeting and if I can attend it I will Accept.

Sending me an email asking if a time is alright increases the communication overhead. If you are unfamiliar with that term then the following definition might help -"Communication overhead refers to the extra time, effort, and resources spent on communication activities instead of getting actual work done". Here we want to book a meeting so please just go ahead and book the meeting on teams, this is the most efficient use of your time and mine.  Remember to invite the attendees, Your client and all your team Members.

Thank you so much.

Best regards Peter
